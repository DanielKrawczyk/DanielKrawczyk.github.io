Dungeons & Dragons campaign

 Version: v0.1.1

 Interactive Dungeons & Dragons campaign. Immerse yourself in the fantastic world of Forgotten Realms and experience your own adventure! Solve the secrets of the Arcane Tower, find out who the mysterious murderer is and travel between Outer Planes to find your destiny! Fully functional equipment and hero advancement system, the ability to save the moment of the game, lots of locations, quests, choices and enemies, as well as music to make the game fun!

 This project use: HTML, SCSS, JavaScript, jQuery, ReactJS, Redux, Recoil.

 WARNING! Before entering the website, make sure the volume is set to optimal level to avoid surprise!

Weather App

 An app as simple as it is useful for downloading weather information from around the world. Enter the name of the city you are interested in and mark it as your favorite so that you don't have to search for it every time! Well, unless you're a huge fan of cleaning up your browser's local memory!

 This project use: HTML, SCSS, JavaScript, ReactJS, Recoil.

Assets List

 Honestly - the site will not be useful to anyone. It was created to facilitate my work with the Zengin Engine graphics engine. It stores assets ( vobs ) from the Gothic game and displays their code, name, and additional and relevant information. The most important thing for me was to quickly find the asset that interests me and to copy the code as quickly as possible without selecting it.

 This project use: HTML, SCSS, JavaScript, ReactJS, React Router, NodeJS, ExpressJS, Joi
